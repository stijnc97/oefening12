Black with a touch of blue

The nice thing about a black design with a touch of blue is that it gives u a smooth clean design with attrication points where the blue touches are.

Do I use this? Yes I certainly use this, and as often as I can! I personally like smootch clean designs, so its not a big suprise that I'm using this technique.

Why and when should u use it? Well its often used in covers of magazine or on posters. Its just when u want to make something or somebody stand out.
